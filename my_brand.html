My Brand: Trove Video Library Manager

Even with today's plethora of streaming services, there are some people (myself included) who prefer to personal copies of all of their favorite media. And while there plenty of programs to manage and organize your collection of .mp3 files, there is no equivalent program for movies and TV shows.

Until Now.
